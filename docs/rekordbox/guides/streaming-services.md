# Introduction

rekordbox can use music files from a music streaming service for DJ performance. By using music files from a streaming service, you can select more tracks for your DJ performance.

- SoundCloud
  To use the SoundCloud streaming service with rekordbox, you need to register for SoundCloud Go+/DJ. For details on services and available areas, visit the SoundCloud website.
  https://soundcloud.com/

- Beatport
  To use the Beatport streaming service with rekordbox, you need to register for Beatport Streaming. For details on services and available areas, visit the Beatport website.
  https://stream.beatport.com/

- Beatsource
  To use the Beatsource streaming service with rekordbox, you need to register for Beatsource Streaming. For details on services and available area, visit the Beatsource website.
  https://www.beatsource.com/

- TIDAL
  To use the TIDAL streaming service with rekordbox, you need to register for TIDAL Premium or TIDAL HiFi. For details on services and available area, visit the TIDAL website.
  https://tidal.com/

- Apple Music
  To use the Apple Music streaming service with rekordbox, you need to register for Apple Music. For details on services and available area, visit the Apple Music website.
  https://www.apple.com/apple-music/

- Spotify
  To use the Spotify streaming service with rekordbox, you need to register for Spotify. For details on services and available area, visit the Spotify website.
  https://open.spotify.com/

To log in to the music streaming service, it is required to connect your computer to the Internet.

# Log in

Check that [**PERFORMANCE**] is selected in the upper-left corner of the screen.

## Displaying music files from SoundCloud

Log in to SoundCloud from rekordbox to display music files from SoundCloud in the track list of the browser panel.

1. Click (SoundCloud icon) in Media Browser and click [Log in].

[Screenshot: Media Browser showing SoundCloud selected with Log in button highlighted]

2. Click [Sign in to your SoundCloud account].
   Your web browser launches and the SoundCloud sign in window is displayed.

3. Log in to an account with SoundCloud Go+/DJ.
   When log in is completed, the SoundCloud library loads onto rekordbox.
   - If you log in to an account without SoundCloud Go+/DJ, tracks cannot be loaded.

   The loaded tracks are indicated with (SoundCloud icon).
   - Tracks indicated with (unavailable icon) cannot be played on rekordbox.

**Hint**

- When you click [Free], you can use tracks in Free DJ Playlists.

### To log out

Right-click [SoundCloud] in the tree view, and then select [Log out].

### Menu in the tree view

[Screenshot: SoundCloud tree view showing numbered menu items: Search Tracks, Genre, Library (with sub-items Likes, Playlists, Albums, Following, History), Recommend (with sub-items Follow Recommend, Streaming Recommend, Artist Recommend), Downloads, and Update button]

1. [Search Tracks]
   Searches a track.

2. [Genre]
   Displays tracks by genre.

3. [Likes]
   Displays tracks set as your favorites.

4. [Playlists]
   Displays playlists created or set as your favorite on SoundCloud.

5. [Albums]
   Displays your favorite albums on SoundCloud.

6. [Following]
   Displays your favorite users on SoundCloud.

7. [History]
   Displays the tracks played on SoundCloud.

8. [Follow Recommend]
   Displays recommended tracks from artists you follow.

9. [Streaming Recommend]
   Displays recommended tracks based on your recently imported tracks.

10. [Artists Recommend]
    Displays recommended tracks from artists based on your recently imported tracks.

11. [Downloads]
    Displays SoundCloud tracks saved on your computer.

12. Update button
    Updates the library.

### [SoundCloud] in the [Preferences] window

By opening the [Preferences] window > [Advanced] category > [Others] tab, you can set the audio quality in [SoundCloud].

[Screenshot: Preferences window showing Advanced > Others tab with Streaming Audio Quality settings for SoundCloud (High Quality 256kbps (AAC), Standard Quality 160kbps or 96kbps (AAC)), Beatport (Lossless (FLAC), High (256 AAC), Standard (128 AAC)), Beatsource (Lossless (FLAC), High (256 AAC), Standard (128 AAC)), and TIDAL (Lossless (FLAC), High (AAC)), plus Automatic removal of auditioned tracks option]

You can use High Quality Streaming by selecting [High Quality 256kbps (AAC)]. However, depending on the track, the selected audio quality may not be available. If Standard Quality is not available, the track will be analyzed and played in High Quality. If High Quality is not available, the track will be analyzed and played in Standard Quality. The BeatGrid and CUE points may also need to be reset due to the possibility of the BeatGrid and CUE points shifting when [Streaming Audio Quality] is changed.

# Playing

## Importing and playing tracks

For playing Spotify tracks, see "Playing Spotify Tracks" (page 27).

1. Right-click the track, and then select [Import To Collection].
   Music files are downloaded and imported to [Collection].
   Imported tracks are indicated with (imported icon).

2. Load the track to the deck, and then play it.

**Notes**

- Before operation, check that your computer is connected to the internet.
- Import tracks one at a time.
- When an un-imported track is loaded to the deck, importing starts automatically.
- While a streaming track is being loaded to the deck, recording is not available.
- For limitations on operating streaming tracks, see "Limitations" (page 36).

### To analyze tracks

If the [Preferences] window > [Analysis] tab > [Track Analysis] > [Auto Analysis] is set to [Enable], analysis of a track automatically starts after the track is imported. To start the analysis, right-click the track, and then select [Analyze Track]. For more details, refer to "Instruction Manual."

### To preview a track

When you click the title or artist of the track displayed in [Genre], the preview player will be displayed. On the preview player, you can play the track before importing it.

[Screenshot: Preview player bar showing play/pause button, time display (00:00), playback position slider, time remaining (00:00), import to collection button, and show/hide button]

1. Starts or pauses playback.

2. Changes the playback position.

3. Imports a track to [Collection].

4. Shows/hides the preview player.

**Hint**

- You can also preview a track by clicking on the track title or artwork in the track list displayed when clicking [view all].

1. Select a track, and then click (download icon).
   You can select multiple tracks.
   The track starts to download. Stored tracks are indicated with (Beatport stored icon).
   When you click (stored tracks list icon), the stored tracks are listed.

## Editing (Editing a playlist of the streaming service)

You can edit a playlist of the streaming service on rekordbox.
For the streaming services that allow playlist editing, check the FAQ provided on the rekordbox website below.
rekordbox.com/en/support/faq/streaming-7/#faq-q700195

[Screenshot: SoundCloud tree view showing Search Tracks, Genre, Library (with Likes, Playlists containing mix-nudisco, mix-disco, My mix, Mixed playlists, Albums, Following, History), with Mixed playlist selected showing "Mixed (115 Tracks)" in track list panel with # and Preview columns showing tracks 1-10. ### Creating a playlist of the streaming service

1. click + next to the playlist or playlist folder in the corresponding tree of the streaming service.
   A new playlist ([Untitled Playlist]) is added.

2. Enter a playlist name from the computer keyboard, and then press the Enter key.

3. Drag and drop a track from the same streaming service into the playlist.
   The track is added to the playlist.

**Hints**

- To delete a playlist, right-click the playlist and select [Delete Playlist]. You can also delete a playlist by pressing the Delete key on the computer keyboard.
- To remove a track from the playlist, right-click the track and select [Remove from Playlist]. You can also remove a track by pressing the Delete key on the computer keyboard.
- You can change the order of tracks in the playlist by dragging and dropping them. However, the order of tracks in the SoundCloud playlist cannot be changed.

# Others

## Troubleshooting

Before making inquiries on operating procedures or technical issues, read the following and check the FAQ provided on rekordbox.com.

### Cannot log in to the music streaming service.

- Check that your computer is connected to the internet.

### Cannot load a track to the deck.

- If a track has not been imported, it is imported before loading. Wait until the import is completed.
- Check that the track a track has not been deleted from the music streaming service.
- While recording, loading is not available.

### The waveform is not displayed in the track list.

- Import the track first, and then analyze it (page 25).

### [SoundCloud], [Beatport], [Beatsource], [TIDAL], [Apple Music], or [Spotify] is not displayed in the tree view.

- Update rekordbox to the latest version.
- Open the [Preferences] window > [View] category and select [SoundCloud], [Beatport], [Beatsource], [TIDAL], [Apple Music], or [Spotify] in the [Layout] tab.
- Cannot be displayed on the sub browser screen.

### The BeatGrid and CUE points shifted after changing the [Streaming Audio Quality].

- Reset the BeatGrid and CUE points.
  Also, refer to the rekordbox website FAQ below.
  rekordbox.com/en/support/faq/streaming-7/#faq-q700146

## Limitations

The following operations are not available for tracks from a music streaming service. Also, the tree view for the streaming service cannot be edited on rekordbox.

- Loading tracks to the sampler slot
- Playing tracks in LINK EXPORT
- USB EXPORT
- Recording
- Extracting sound from the enlarged waveform (CAPTURE)
- Using the backup function
- Editing track information

## Copyright warning

The use of rekordbox is restricted with regard to the playing and copying of copy-protected music.
